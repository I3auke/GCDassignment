The output of the run_analysis.R script is has the filename "avg_act_subj.txt"

The file is a table with 4 columns and 11882 rows
below, a description of the columns:

* 1 subjects
unit: numeric-label
Is the integer number 1 to 30 identifying the 30 people that participated in the experiment 

* 2 activity_label
unit: label 
Is the string that shows the activity during the measurements: 6 labels from "sitting" to "laying" 

* 3 variable
unit: string
a description of the variable of which the average shown in the 'mean' column

* 4 mean
Is the mean of the measurements of the variable in the column 'variable' for that specific subject/activity combination
